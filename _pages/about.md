<!-- ## About Me -->

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, advised by Professor [Yin Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=_y-8nrcAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate).

Previously, I obtained a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of Southern California, where I was advised by Professor [Ram Nevatia](https://sites.usc.edu/iris-cvlab/professor-ram-nevatia/). Before that, I earned my bachelor’s degree from Sun Yat-sen University.

My research interests include Computer Vision and Machine Learning, with a particular focus on Vision-Language Learning and Video Understanding.
